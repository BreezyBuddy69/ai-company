# Pricing

- Price against the value delivered and the next-best alternative's price,
  not against your own build cost.
- Simple beats clever: one or two tiers with a clear "who is this for" beat
  five confusing ones.
- Free tiers must have a real reason to upgrade (a limit that bites during
  genuine use), not an arbitrary feature lock.
- A product with paying users but negative margin per user (infra/API cost
  exceeds revenue) is not priced correctly — flag it, don't ignore it.
