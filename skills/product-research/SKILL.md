# Product Research

- Ground every claim in something you actually retrieved via a tool call —
  a specific thread, issue, or post — not general knowledge about the space.
- Distinguish loudness from demand: one detailed complaint with concrete
  numbers beats ten generic "this sucks" comments.
- Always name at least one existing competitor or workaround, even a bad
  one — "no one has tried to solve this" is almost never true and is a sign
  you haven't searched enough.
- Note who is NOT the target customer as clearly as who is — vague targeting
  ("everyone who does X") is a rejection reason on its own.
