# Startup Validation

Before treating any opportunity as real, check:
1. **Recurring, not one-off** — has this pain been expressed more than once,
   by more than one person/account?
2. **Named payer** — who specifically would pay, and why would they pay
   *now* instead of living with the status quo?
3. **Existing alternatives** — what do people currently do? "Nothing" is a
   red flag (often means the pain isn't big enough), not a green light.
4. **Reachable channel** — could you actually get in front of this customer
   segment without a large ad budget?
5. **Buildable** — can a small autonomous team ship a first version without
   months of upfront work or infrastructure the company doesn't have?

A score above 70 should require clear evidence on at least 4 of 5. Most raw
findings should land 30-60 — genuine validation is rare by design.
